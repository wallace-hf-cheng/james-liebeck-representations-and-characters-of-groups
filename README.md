# james-liebeck-representations-and-characters-of-groups
My notes and solutions to some of the exercises in Gordon James's and Martin Liebeck's textbook "Representations and Characters of Groups".

## Details about the book
- Title: Representations and Characters of Groups
- Author: Gordon James and Martin Liebeck
- Edition: Second edition
- Publisher: Cambridge University Press
- Year: 2012
- ISBN: 978-0-511-81453-2
- DOI: 10.1017/CBO9780511814532
- Website: https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/representations-and-characters-of-groups/9F525E6ACAC7FFADFDBDECE98C115F40

## About the solutions
- The emphasis of these solutions is on the main mathematical ideas rather than routine calculations. Consequently, straightforward computational details are occasionally omitted. For lengthy or mechanical computations, a computer algebra system (primarily SymPy) may be used. Whenever such computations play a role in a solution, the corresponding code is included.
